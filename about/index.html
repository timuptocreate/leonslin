About Us

About us

Leonslin was founded in 2003 as a creative agency with focus on Photo and Video Production.

Over the years we have gained substantial expertise by working on a diverse range of Projects that vary in size and standards. While our primary focus has been on Photo and Video Production, we also got a proven track record in successfully delivering an array of other professional services, including Product Display, Brand Identity Design, Product Packaging Design, Catalog Design, Art Exhibition Management, 3d Animations, Web Design, and H5.

Leonslin is a Shanghai-born company Delivering Media to different Clients, all over the Globe with the BEST Photo/Video Production Their Money Can Buy.

Understanding that every project is unique, Leonslin customizes each to fit the client’s communications needs, goals, personality, audience, and production budget.

Appreciating our clients’ fiscal demands, Leonslin designs, plans and executes our productions efficiently to fit the project’s scope and budget as neatly as possible.

Leonslin’s creative teams are masters of content development and acquisition: From pre- through post-production, our teams possess the skills to produce quality work, making both Leonslin and our valued clients proud.

Whether the project is a long-form, institutional or corporate video, an engaging social media campaign, or a series of stunning photographs. We welcome the opportunity to work with new and returning clients to create powerful and creative media regardless of shoot location and/or production challenges.

WE DELIVER ON TIME: We make deadlines a priority and we stick to them. We understand the importance of your meeting, your trade show or your premier, and will work around the clock to deliver the goods.

NO HIDDEN COSTS: We don’t make our money by making it after the fact, we make it by making our clients happy. We are known for our clear, up-front and detailed proposals that help our clients understand exactly what they’re paying for and why. Expect to spend what you signed up for and get more than you paid for.

Pre-Production is SIMPLE: We transcribe all interviews to get us all on the same page right from the start. This makes whole Production Process easier to track and execute.

WE ARE HERE FOR YOU: Our communications are extremely timely and we pride ourselves on same day response to questions or concerns.
